ABOUT CLEAN SLATE

Clean Slate Production is a professional video production company based in London, founded by Edan Schiller.

At Clean Slate, we work with brands, independent businesses, corporates and charities offering creative, production and post production services. With over 12 years in the field our services include marketing videos, fashion films, educational films, documentaries, music videos, training videos and more

CLEAN SLATE looks at each project as a chance to nurture great ideas from concept to completion. We understand that each project is different, and as such, a team of professionals will be hired in accordance with each project, to bring any kind of video production to perfection.

EDAN SCHILLER

Edan Schiller, an experienced director, editor and cinematographer, created CLEAN SLATE in order to give his clients a service that includes all stages of production in a cost effective way from concept development, to production and editing.
